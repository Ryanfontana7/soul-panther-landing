Bio
Soul
Panther
Electronic · Cinematic

I make music to make you feel deeply alive.

I spent years touring the world chasing the high — then I woke up, walked away, and came back with one intention: to bring soul back into dance music.

I chased that mission across every sound I could find. It led me here — to Temple Trap: handpan and ancient instruments over 808s and modern beats. Hypnotic, percussive, primal — ancient texture woven through cinematic electronic music, built to move your body and the deeper parts of you at once.

A balance of opposites — ancient and modern, sacred and primal, ethereal yet deep.

This is dance music with a soul, for the sacred rebels — the ones who refuse to choose between the spiritual and the visceral. Welcome home.
